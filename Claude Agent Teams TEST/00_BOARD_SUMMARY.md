## Board Consensus: The Emerging Strategy

### What the Board Killed
- ❌ Original $29/month business plan generator — unanimously dead
- ❌ Visionary's equity/success fee model — Legal scored 2/10 (securities law, $500K-1.5M/yr compliance)
- ❌ "Everyone" as target audience
- ❌ Subscription on a one-shot utility

### What the Board Endorsed
- ✅ **Repositioning: "AI Startup Validation"** (Marketing Genius, backed by 5/7 agents)
- ✅ **"Conviction, not documents"** as core value prop (Creative + Marketing, endorsed by all)
- ✅ **Deterministic financial engine** — LLM extracts assumptions, code does math (Tech Nerd, endorsed by Legal as liability shield)
- ✅ **Shareable "Startup Score"** as viral artifact (Marketing + Creative)
- ✅ **Phased evolution:** Validation → AI Co-Founder → Network Intelligence (Analyst's sequencing)
- ✅ **Dark, opinionated brand** with "Brutally Honest Mentor" voice (Creative Producer)

### The Recommended Path

**Phase 0 (Weeks 1-4):** Free viral score tool. One question → AI probes deeper → 5-dimension Startup Score with paced reveal. Shareable dark-mode card. Hook: "Your startup has a 92% chance of failing. Want to know why?"

**Phase 1 (Months 2-6):** Paid tiers. Free (score only), $99 one-time (full validation report), $49/month (ongoing validation). Target: first-time technical founders in a specific vertical.

**Phase 2 (Months 6-18):** AI Co-Founder features. Persistent memory, live data, scenario modeling. $149-299/month. Only after validating retention.

**Phase 3 (Year 2+):** Network intelligence flywheel. Anonymized validation data → proprietary market intelligence. Sell to investors/accelerators. This is the moat.

### Unresolved Tensions
1. **Visionary vs Legal:** "Pre-company stack" vision scored 8/10 (Creative) but 2/10 (Legal)
2. **Tech vs Board:** Tech says "build fast, validate." Business says "validate positioning first."
3. **Pricing:** Analyst favors $99/month (best LTV:CAC). Marketing wants free + $99 one-time + upsell.

---

## Final Board Recommendation

**DO NOT build the original pitch.** Unanimously rejected.

**DO build "AI Startup Validation"** as entry wedge, phased roadmap toward "AI Co-Founder."

**The single most important decision:** Make the Startup Score shareable and brutal. If it goes viral, the company works. If it doesn't, nothing else matters.
